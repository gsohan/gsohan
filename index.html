Sohan Gyawali

Assistant Professor

Department of Technology Systems

East Carolina University



Sohan Gyawali received a Ph.D. degree in Computer Engineering from University of Nebraska-Lincoln (UNL) in 2020. He is currently an Assistant Professor in the Department of Technology Systems, East Carolina University. His research interests include misbehavior detection, cybersecurity, cellular based vehicular communications, machine learning and intelligent transportation systems. He is a member of the IEEE and IEEE communications society.

Education

  • Ph.D. in Computer Engineering, Aug 2020, University of Nebraska-Lincoln, Omaha, NE, USA
    • Dissertation: Misbehavior Detection and Privacy in Cellular Based Vehicular Communication Networks
    • Supervisor: Professor Yi Qian
  • B.E. in Electronics and Communications, Aug 2014, Tribhuvan University, Kathmandu, Nepal

Research Interests

  • Network security: network intelligence, privacy and lightweight encryption.
  • Cellular based vehicular communications: vehicular fog computing, edge computing and software defined networking.
  • Machine learning: supervised, semi-supervised, unsupervised learning, deep reinforcement learning, online learning.
  • Intrusion and misbehavior detection: machine learning and reputation-based detection.
